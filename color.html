Unit 2 Project

The project in this unit was based on color of web pages. I chose 2 fiction clients and chose a color pallete for each. I selected each color pallete to represent and reflect the purpose and ideas of each client.

Bank's WebPage

Explanation

Banks are associated with strong and safe places. To reflect these characteristics in a color scheme, I chose strong and bold colors. At the same time these colors can show luxury, another important characteristic of a bank.

Landscape Architect's WebPage

Explanation

To understand this color scheme we need to understand the purpose of a landscape architect. This architect works with nature and uses it to design decorations. This color scheme reflects this with natural colors that can be found in nature.
