Unit 1: HTML

  1. What are the most important vocabulary terms in this lesson and what do they mean?
    • HTML- a type of computing language that is used to edit text
    • code- edit a document using and editor with HTML
    • tags- opening and closing mark that that determines type of text

  2. What are the most important skills that I learned in this lesson?
    • I learned to edit text using HTML.

  3. In what ways have I gotten better at this kind of work?
    • I started working with HTML for the first time and learned the
      basics.

  4. In what ways do I need to improve?
    • I want to master using HTML by knowing and memorizing every
      kind of tags and fetaures available in HTML.

  5. What problems did I encounter while working on this piece and how did I solve them?
    • The main problem that I encountered was using complex tags like
      "mailto" and the "main id". I solved these problems by requesting
      help from Mr. Lanus.

    • I enjoyed the whole proccess of editing using Atom and the final

    • I would say that I did a good job because I followed all directions.

Unit 2: Color

  1. What are the most important vocabulary terms in this lesson and what do they mean?
    • color scheme- Color or group of colors used in the design of
      a website

  2. What are the most important skills that I learned in this lesson?
    • I learned to create and choose the right color scheme to fit the
      web page's purpose.

  3. In what ways have I gotten better at this kind of work?
    • I now understand and know how to use hexidecimals.

  4. In what ways do I need to improve?

  5. What problems did I encounter while working on this piece and how did I solve them?
    • It was challenging to choose the right color shades but I solved it
      by asking for help.

  6. What​ ​was​ ​especially​ ​satisfying​ ​to​ ​me​ ​about​ ​either​ ​the​ ​process​ ​or​ ​the​ ​finished​ ​product?

      looking good.

Unit 3: Typography

  1. What are the most important vocabulary terms in this lesson and what do they mean?
    • kerning- spacing between letters
    • leading- spacing between lines
    • hierarchy- using font-weight, font-family, and font size to make
      some text appear more important
    • contrast- editing text to make it visible against its background

  2. What are the most important skills that I learned in this lesson?
    • I learned how to edit text applying hierarchy and contrast to make
      text stand out in front of a background image.

  3. In what ways have I gotten better at this kind of work?
    • I got better at designing cool images using color anf fonts.

  4. In what ways do I need to improve?
    • I want to get better at creating contrast and using better coloring.

  5. What problems did I encounter while working on this piece and how did I solve them?
    • I couldn't create enough contrast with some darker images.

  6. What​ ​was​ ​especially​ ​satisfying​ ​to​ ​me​ ​about​ ​either​ ​the​ ​process​ ​or​ ​the​ ​finished​ ​product?
    • I really liked how my two quotes turned out with enough color,
      cool fonts, and contrast

  7. If I was the teacher, what comments would I make about this piece?
    • I would say that I did a very good job because it shows creativity.
